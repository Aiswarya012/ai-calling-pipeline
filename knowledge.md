# Sharma Motors — Showroom Knowledge Base

Showroom hours: Monday to Saturday, 10:00 AM to 7:00 PM. Closed on Sundays.

Location: Sharma Motors, MG Road, Bengaluru. Free customer parking is available.

We sell new and certified pre-owned cars. Brands available: Maruti Suzuki, Hyundai, Tata, and Mahindra.

Popular models and on-road prices: Maruti Swift starts at 7.5 lakh. Hyundai Creta starts at 12 lakh. Tata Nexon starts at 9 lakh. Mahindra Scorpio-N starts at 15 lakh.

Test drives: Free test drives are available for all models. Bring a valid driving license. Test drives can be booked over the phone or by walking in.

Finance and loans: We offer car loans through HDFC, ICICI, and SBI. Loan tenure up to 7 years. Down payment starts from 10 percent of the on-road price.

Exchange: We accept old car exchange. Free valuation of your old car is done at the showroom.

Service and warranty: New cars come with a 3-year manufacturer warranty. We have an in-house service center for periodic maintenance and repairs.

Appointments: For test drives or sales appointments, collect the customer's name, preferred date, and time. Confirm the car model they are interested in.
